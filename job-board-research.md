## High-Priority Gaps

### 1. Getro / Jobs in VC (jobsinvc.getro.com)
- Powers 700+ VC fund job boards, aggregated into one searchable interface
- Covers 500 Global, 8VC, 645 Ventures, Kapor Capital, and hundreds more
- Single biggest coverage gap vs current setup

### 2. TrueUp (trueup.io)
- Aggregator: 15,000+ early-stage startup roles across 1,900+ companies
- Filters for pre-unicorn startups, salary data, company growth metrics

### 3. GTMfund Job Board (jobs.gtmfund.com)
- B2B SaaS-focused VC, 350+ GTM executives in network
- Portfolio board frequently has CS/CX leadership roles

### 4. TopStartups.io (topstartups.io)
- Aggregates across Sequoia, a16z, Benchmark, Accel, Bessemer portfolios
- Filter by investor, funding round, industry

## CX/CS-Specific Boards

### 5. CS Insider (csinsider.co)
- Dedicated Customer Success job board and community

### 6. TalentWay (talentway.com)
- AI-powered daily CSM job matches, scans 10+ boards and CS communities

### 7. Aspireship (aspireship.com)
- SaaS CS training + placement platform, companies hire directly

### 8. CXPA Career Center (cxpaglobal.org)
- Customer Experience Professionals Association job board
